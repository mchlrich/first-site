Driving Factors

Each of the images above are all important pieces of my life in some form or fashion. They all have something in common as well. Can you guess what it is?


Muscle memory. The human brain is similar to a machine in many respects, but the ability to learn consistently from repeating the same actions isn't something a machine can do without us. This is how people can still arrive at the correct place, even if another path is taken than what another individual may choose to get there. We can also improve that process, again through repetition. Muscle memory is the single greatest aspect of developing the how, and we improve our knowledge of the world around us by asking a simple why... Over and over and over again!
